Hello! I'm Margaret Gitau, a passionate and self-driven web developer with a background in Finance. My journey into the world of technology began out of curiosity, and it has since grown into a deep commitment to building functional, user-friendly websites that serve real-world needs.

Although I hold a Bachelor of Commerce degree in Finance from Mount Kenya University, I discovered my love for technology and problem-solving through code — a passion that led me to pursue software development at the PLP Academy. I’ve since created several web pages and personal projects, including a cooking and baking blog, an eCommerce baby shop website called Carey Baby Shop, and my own professional portfolio.

I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies to bring ideas to life in the browser. I believe in continuous learning and strive to grow my skills daily through new challenges, online resources, and real-world projects.

Beyond coding, I’m also enthusiastic about helping others, contributing to communities, and using technology to make a positive impact. I’m currently focused on expanding my front-end skills and exploring responsive design, interactivity, and modern frameworks.
